We left our design jobs to make lights that feel like home

After a decade designing for big furniture brands, we got tired of seeing the same mass-produced fixtures in every catalog. So we rented a small workshop in Sonoma, bought a kiln and a lathe, and started casting brass by hand.

Two years later, we're still here — still hand-finishing every piece, still refusing to cut corners, still convinced that the right light can change the way a room feels.

— Mara & Leo, founders
